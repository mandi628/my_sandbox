Dr. Theresa Aumann

The woman who climbed volcanoes

Timeline

  • 2005: Born in Kennesaw, GA to Mark & Amanda Aumann, youngest of four children.
  • 2007: Moved to Anderson, SC with the family.
  • 2012: Entered public school after homeschooling the first couple of years.
  • 2015: After casually playing violin with mom, Theresa began her viola studies in the public school.
  • 2016: Entered the fine arts middle school.
  • 2021: After attempting online options during the COVID-19 Pandemic, Theresa went back to homeschooling until her senior year of high school.
  • 2023: Graduated from TL Hanna High School in Anderson, SC. She began her undergraduate studies at Susquehannah University in Selinsgrove, PA.
  • 2027: After graduating with honors from both her departments, Theresa continued her studies with a MS Seismology from Yale University in New Haven, CT. During this time, she worked closely with research specialists in the volcanology field.
  • 2029: Theresa began her PhD work with researchers from Harvard University in Cambridge, MA.
